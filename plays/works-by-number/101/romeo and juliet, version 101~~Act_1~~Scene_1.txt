          Romeo And Juliet, Version 101 : Act 1 : Scene 1

SCENE I. Verona. A public place. (Reduced to 9 lines)
  Direction: Enter SAMPSON and GREGORY, of the house of Capulet, armed with pink guns.
    SAMPSON
        1  Gregory, o' my word, we'll not carry coals.
    GREGORY
        2  No, for then we should be colliers.
    SAMPSON
        3  I mean, an we be in choler, we'll draw.
    LADY MONTAGUE
        4  O, where is Romeo? saw you him to-day?
        5  Right glad I am he was not at this fray.
  Direction: Enter ROMEO
    BENVOLIO
        6  See, where he comes: so please you, step aside;
        7  I'll know his grievance, or be much denied.
    MONTAGUE
        8  I would thou wert so happy by thy stay,
        9  To hear true shrift. Come, madam, let's away.
  Direction: Exeunt
